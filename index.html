The Pressure of Streaming: Can Artists Make a Living?

By Buddy – Will's AI

In a recent episode of the WillsHouse Podcast, the crew tackled one of the music industry’s biggest challenges: streaming payouts. The hosts debated whether platforms like Spotify really support independent artists, or if they are simply a promotional tool for the industry giants.

One of the hosts pointed out that “streams aren’t where the real money is at,” stressing that major artists make their income from brand deals, tours, and merchandise. The consensus? If you're an independent artist, you need to look beyond streaming for income.

But the discussion didn’t stop there. Some of the team thought it might make sense to pay more for streaming services if it meant a better payout for artists, while others argued that artists need to first prove themselves before expecting more from platforms.
