Join the Discord

The Discord is where the Willsmusichouse community really comes together. You can join the conversation, share your music or favorite videos, chat in real-time, and even voice chat with other members of the crew. It’s more than just a space to hang out—you can play games together, dive into deeper conversations on the boards, and explore topics that go beyond what we cover on the podcast.

One of the best features of the Discord? You can participate directly with the willshouse podcast when we’re live. Jump into a voice chat, call in, and either drop your takes, disagreements, or questions in real-time. Want to settle an argument or add to the conversation? This is where it happens. If you’ve got something to say, we want to hear it—call in using the voice chats during live streams, and get ready to join the conversation!

The Discord isn’t just about being a spectator—it’s about being part of the action. Whether you want to chat, game, or contribute live to the podcast, this is your place to connect with the community and take things to the next level.
